  - Form possessives with ◌ගේ (gé) for living things; exceptions: මගේ (magē)
  - Say “My name is …” / “I am …”
  - Ask “What is your name?”
  - Use “thank you” to reply politely
prerequisites: [unit-01]

assessment: Self-intro dialogue with polite closing.
